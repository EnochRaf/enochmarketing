Our Story

BORN IN THE GYM.
BUILT FOR GYM OWNERS.

Enoch Marketing started with a simple observation: gym owners were getting burned by agencies that didn't understand fitness. They'd hire a "marketing guy" who ran the same playbook they used for dentists and restaurants — and wonder why nobody was walking through the door.

We knew there had to be a better way. So we built an agency that does one thing and does it obsessively: grow gyms. Every strategy, every campaign, every funnel we build is designed specifically for the way people find, evaluate, and join a gym.

We've helped gyms across the country fill their floors, pack their classes, and build the kind of brand that turns first-timers into lifers.
